"Hiraia" is derived from the Tagalog phrase "hiraya manawari,"
which means "sana matupad." It can also mean "to reach for your dreams."
Combining my pen name "Raia Saionji" with "hiraya" means to reach for MY dreams.
It is a promise and a challenge to myself.
To always reach for perfection, beauty, and my goals.
I am an aspiring artist, designer, and writer.
Ever since, the realm of arts and fantasy is always a curious topic for me
which made me feel a deep connection with this craft.
